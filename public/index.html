Our Privacy Philosophy

At TabVault AI, we believe your browser tabs are your personal business. Unlike other AI tools, we never upload your data to external servers. Your tab titles, URLs, and browsing habits stay exactly where they belong: on your device.

Local AI Analysis

We use Chrome's built-in AI engine (Gemini Nano) to categorize your tabs. All processing is 100% local, air-gapped from any cloud network.

No Tracking

We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or cookies. We don't know who you are, how many tabs you have, or what you're browsing.

Permission Transparency

Tabs: Required only to read titles/URLs for categorization.
TabGroups: Required to physically organize tabs into groups.
Storage: Used to save your local UI preferences only.
